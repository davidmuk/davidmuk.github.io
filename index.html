Design Thinking Process

An iterative and collaborative design thinking methodology enables you to rapidly find solutions for your problems. The process is founded on insight - analysing and questioning everything - positioning the experiences in human-centric ways.

Empathise

The first stage is to gain a thorough and empathic understanding of your business, your customers and your problems. With insight we can uncover new ways to meet your customer needs.

Define

We analyse all the information gathered during the previous stage so that we can define the human centered problems. From this analysis we start to gather initial ideas and solutions.

Ideate

To answer the problems we've uncovered we collaboratively brainstorm, whiteboard and workshop new ideas, solutions and functions.

 

Prototype

This is an experimental phase, and the aim is to identify the best possible solution for the problems we have identified. We develop rapid, interactive prototypes from which we can quickly test and refine, or even generate new solutions and ideas.

Evolve

We test the prototypes - the results can redefine problems and continually inform our understanding of your customers.

 

 

 

Collaborate

A visual collaborative platform ensures all the team can contribute effectively - improving workflows and transparency. With fast and easy access to knowledge you can be agile and iterate quickly.
